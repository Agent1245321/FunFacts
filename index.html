Fun Fact of the Day!

The invention of tea is believed to have happened in 2737 BCE when a servant of the Chinese emperor, Shen Nung, was boiling water under a tree. A strong wind blew leaves off the tree and into the boiling water, the servant thought the water was ruined and was going to throw it away, the emperor however was a skilled herbalist who knew the leaf wouldn't make the water poisonous and requested the servant keep the water so he could try it.
