Fun Fact of the Day!!!

Fun Fact: "Who's Afraid of Red, Yellow, and Blue is a series of four large paintings by famous artist Barnett Newman. They were painted between 1966 and 1970. Two of the four paintings have suffered vandalism over the years, one of which being cut to pieces. People attacked the paints for seemingly being easy to create and thus having no artistic value. Despite this none of the many attempts to recreate the painting has ever been successful"
