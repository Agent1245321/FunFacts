Fun Fact of the Day!!!

The reason splashing cold water on your face, and more specifically your eyes, causes you to wake up is that when water gets on or around your eyes it makes your body think that you might be drowning activating your sympathetic nervous system, the part of your brain responsible for fight or flight responses.
