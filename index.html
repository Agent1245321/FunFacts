Fun Fact of the Day!!!

Fun Fact: "'E.T. the Extra Terrestrial', was a tie in game for the movie of the same name. Atari only gave the developer around five weeks to finish the game resulting in it being a terrible game. The game was, in fact, so bad that it's widely credited as one of the causes of the video game crash of 1983. The game caused massive losses for Atari, eventually an urban legend sprung up that the game sold so poorly they had all remaining copies buried in the Mojave Desert in New Mexico. This was nothing more than an urban legend until, in 2014 excavators investigating the legend found copies of E.T. as well as thousands of other old Atari games all buried in the desert"
