Fun Fact of the Day!

The oldest written story, The Epic of Gilgamesh, dating all the way back to 2100 BCE was first discovered in the ruins of a destroyed library. The library had been burned down during a political uprising baking the clay tablets the story was written on preserving them forever but burying the pieces under the rubble of the library.
