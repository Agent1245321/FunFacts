Fun Fact of the Day!

Fun Fact: "The ancient Inca people had a record keeping system called khipu. Khipu were a series of ropes that were tied and knotted in a certain way to be able to contain information. Even things like the color of rope and the direction they rotated could be used to communicate. At first modern scientist thought they could only be used for counting but after more recent study it was determined that they could be used to communicate all sorts of things that we can simply no longer translate"
